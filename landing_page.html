My dearest Anvita,

It’s hard to believe that today marks our first anniversary as a couple. When I think back to 19th October 2023, when we were just strangers, I never could have imagined that one day, you would become the most important person in my life. Our connection started quietly, then grew steadily from 14th January 2024, when we began talking more, laughing more, and slowly turning strangers into friends. And then, somehow, almost without realizing it, we became best friends—the people we couldn’t go a day without talking to, the ones whose presence, even from afar, made life feel whole.
We shared our highs and lows, our silly stories, our fears, and our dreams. And then came 13th February 2025, the day I gathered the courage to propose to you. I knew my heart was yours, but your hesitation made me respect you even more. When you finally said yes on 26th February 2025, it felt like the universe had aligned perfectly for us. That day was the start of something even more beautiful—we officially became us, while still remaining the best friends at the core of our hearts.
Being in a long-distance relationship hasn’t been easy, but it has shown me how strong what we have truly is. Every message, every call, every video call, every “good morning”, every "good night" made the distance feel smaller. And then, 6th December 2025, when I finally met you… I’ll never forget it. Those two days with you were the happiest of my life—the first hug, our first kiss, holding your hand, feeling your presence… it felt like home. It felt like I had been missing a part of myself, and finally found it in you.
Today, as we celebrate one year together, I want you to know this: my heart is yours, completely and forever. I want to continue laughing with you, dreaming with you, growing with you. I want all our tomorrows, all our adventures, all the quiet nights and loud mornings, with you by my side. You’re not just my girlfriend—you’re my best friend, my home, my everything.
Thank you for saying yes, for trusting me, and for letting me love you. Here’s to forever, my love. I can’t wait for the countless years ahead with you, because life with you is everything I’ve ever wanted and more.

Forever yours,

Keshav
